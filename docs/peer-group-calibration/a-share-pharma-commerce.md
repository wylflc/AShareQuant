# 1. A-Share Pharma Commerce Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `医药生物-医药商业-医药商业`.

Pharmaceutical commerce is usually a lower-margin channel, logistics, or retail-network business. Distribution scale alone is not enough for watchlist inclusion. The review therefore separates ordinary regional distribution from integrated pharmaceutical industrial platforms, scarce drug-discovery technology platforms, regulated national distribution platforms, leading pharmacy chains, differentiated commercialization platforms, and diagnostics leaders.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain companies when the company-level capability is actually an integrated pharmaceutical industrial platform, a large pharma industrial-commercial platform, or a scarce drug-discovery technology platform.
2. Retain boundary cases only when they own regulated national distribution scale, scarce product qualification, leading pharmacy-chain consolidation, strong medical-brand commercialization, diagnostics leadership, or branded drug innovation optionality.
3. Reject ordinary regional distribution, low-margin drug/device logistics, weaker regional pharmacy chains, IVD distributors, and mixed healthcare-commerce assets when stronger retained peers cover the durable thesis.
4. Cross-industry capability overrides the industry label; 成都先导 is retained under drug-discovery technology rules, not ordinary pharma-commerce rules.
