# 1. A-Share Medical Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the completion review for the still-unreviewed companies in `医药生物-医疗服务-医疗服务`.

The group mixes specialist medical chains, third-party medical laboratories, genomics platforms, clinical CRO and data-management services, dental and neurosurgery hospitals, physical examination chains, weak duplicate eye hospitals, single hospital assets, medical cleanroom/engineering providers, small CROs, hospital management businesses, and ST cases. Existing CXO decisions already cover `药明康德`, `泰格医药`, and `昭衍新药`.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own a national specialist chain, third-party laboratory platform, or genomics/testing platform with doctor/customer/data/quality-system advantages.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have a narrower but real specialist chain, clinical-service network, dental/neurosurgery specialty, health-check network, or clinical data/statistics niche not fully covered by stronger peers.
3. Reject single or weak hospital assets, weaker duplicate eye hospitals, medical cleanroom/engineering providers, small CROs covered by stronger platforms, ST cases, and low-barrier healthcare management services.
4. For medical services, scale alone is insufficient; the key question is whether doctor network, customer trust, data scale, accreditation, quality systems, or brand create repeatable barriers.
