# 1. A-Share Remaining Rare Small Metals Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for the remaining companies in `有色金属-稀有金属-其他稀有小金属`.

Some companies in this peer group were already decided in strategic resources, lithium resources, and battery materials. This pass covers the remaining companies and applies the same resource-calibration rule: current earnings and commodity cycles are secondary to scarce resource access, processing capability, qualification, and material technology.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they are national uranium platforms, vanadium-titanium strategic-resource platforms, leading precious-metal material and recycling platforms, tantalum-niobium material platforms, broad titanium leaders, or globally relevant molybdenum platforms.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have meaningful mine rights, vanadium-titanium beneficiation capability, listed silver scarcity, aerospace titanium specialization, diversified research-institute materials capability, nano-metal powder process technology, zirconium/advanced-ceramic capability, or zirconium-chain exposure.
3. Reject weak lithium, cobalt, and transformation-material stories when stronger retained resource or battery-material platforms already cover the thesis.
4. For rare metals, a low baseline score can be wrong when it underweights resource scarcity, national security relevance, aerospace qualification, or hard material process technology.
