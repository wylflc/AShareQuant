# 1. A-Share Other General Machinery Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `机械设备-通用设备-其他通用机械`.

The group is a mixed bucket covering global thermal-management parts, lithium-battery equipment, high-end mechanical seals, turbomachinery, filter presses, harmonic reducers, vacuum equipment, cryogenic/LNG equipment, compressor systems, pumps, special bearings, tank containers, superhard tools, liquid cooling, magnetic-levitation turbomachinery, logistics automation, ordinary pump/valve/bearing/gear/tool companies, project automation, ST cases, and mixed machinery manufacturers.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own clear category leadership, safety-critical customer qualification, high-end process know-how, global scale, or scarce core components that a capital-only entrant cannot quickly replicate.
2. Retain boundary cases when they own a narrower but real niche in liquid cooling, cryogenic equipment, compressor systems, pumps, precision bearings, tank containers, powder handling, wind safety equipment, superhard tools, vacuum process equipment, or magnetic-levitation machinery.
3. Reject ordinary pumps, valves, bearings, gears, compressors, tools, molds, logistics systems, project automation, small parts, generic machinery, ST cases, and mixed manufacturing companies without clear category leadership or scarce qualification.
4. Do not let a generic-machinery label hide cross-industry leaders: thermal-management, lithium equipment, mechanical seals, vacuum equipment, and harmonic reducers are retained because their actual capability is not generic.
