# 1. A-Share Wire And Cable Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电气设备-输变电设备-电线电缆`.

The group mixes submarine-cable leaders, high-voltage cable-accessory specialists, UHV transformer material suppliers, aerospace/nuclear/industrial special-cable companies, broad regional cable manufacturers, ordinary construction and power-cable producers, electronic-wire suppliers, and mixed portfolios where cable is no longer the main moat. The review separates qualification-heavy product capability from revenue scale and ordinary cable capacity.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they have scarce submarine-cable capability, high-voltage cable-accessory technology, multi-capability cable/material/operating platforms, or high-end special-cable platforms serving aerospace, nuclear, marine, rail, and national-engineering customers.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have a narrower but visible qualification-heavy niche, such as GIL/modular transmission systems, UHV transformer electromagnetic wire, special cables, industrial export certifications, aerospace optical-electric cables, nuclear special cables, or high-voltage grid project references.
3. Reject ordinary regional cable makers, construction and power-cable capacity, roll-up cable groups, broad cable conglomerates, and fast-growing application-cable suppliers when the moat is mainly capacity, channel, local brand, or end-market growth.
4. Revenue scale and current profit are not enough. A watched company must show evidence that a well-funded new entrant could not quickly replicate the customer qualification, project references, reliability history, product process, or operating network.
