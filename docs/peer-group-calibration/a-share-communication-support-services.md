# 1. A-Share Communication Support Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `信息技术-通信设备-通信配套服务`.

The group mixes cellular IoT modules, quantum communication, special communication and BeiDou/satellite systems, telecom/power surge protection, communication construction, network operations and maintenance, value-added telecom/SMS channels, system integration, IDC/energy themes, and consumer device repair services. The review separates hard communication capability from service-delivery scale.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own scarce cellular-IoT module leadership or quantum communication capability with real technology, certification, and customer-reference barriers.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have special communication, BeiDou/satellite, defense/public-sector qualification, or communication/power protection-component reliability niches.
3. Reject communication construction, network maintenance, SMS/value-added telecom channels, generic system integration, consumer repair, broad IDC/energy themes, and project-heavy service businesses without hard communication IP or platform control.
4. Do not treat revenue scale in communication O&M as a moat by itself; a funded competitor can usually hire delivery teams and pursue operator relationships, so service scale must be paired with product/IP barriers to justify inclusion.
