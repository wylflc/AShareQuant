# 1. A-Share B2B E-Commerce Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `互联网-互联网商务-电子商务B2B`.

The group mixes industrial B2B platforms, commodity data and pricing platforms, cross-border B2B marketplaces, electronic-component supply-chain platforms, cross-border brand operators, e-commerce brand-service providers, brand-licensing models, cashback and marketing platforms, and legacy e-commerce companies. The review separates platform and data moats from traffic, service, and trading models.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when industrial transaction networks, commodity data, pricing workflow, and supply-chain services reinforce each other.
2. Retain boundary cases when a company has a real cross-border B2B buyer-supplier network, vertical electronic-component procurement data, or a differentiated cross-border brand and supply-chain system.
3. Reject e-commerce代运营, brand-authorization models, cashback and traffic platforms, legacy cross-border e-commerce turnarounds, mixed manufacturing and e-commerce companies, and industrial IT service providers without platform network effects.
4. A watched B2B e-commerce company must show data lock-in, transaction network effects, procurement workflow integration, supply-chain service depth, or brand/supply-chain capability that is harder to copy than buying traffic or adding SKU volume.
