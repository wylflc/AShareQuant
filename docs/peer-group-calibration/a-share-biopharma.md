# 1. A-Share Biopharma Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `医药生物-生物医药-生物医药`.

The group mixes global innovative drugs, biologics, vaccines, plasma products, IVD, companion diagnostics, life-science tools, bioactive materials, and early pipeline companies. Current profit is not the primary screen; the review focuses on clinical/regulatory validation, scarce biological resources, platform science, product workflow ownership, and whether a new entrant could replicate the position with capital alone.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain global or clearly differentiated innovative-drug platforms when clinical assets, regulatory path, BD validation, or commercial proof supports a durable capability.
2. Retain scarce biological-resource platforms such as plasma products when plasma-station/resource constraints and regulatory barriers are company-specific.
3. Retain IVD, companion-diagnostic, life-science-tool, or biomaterial companies only when the workflow, installed base, productization, or process know-how is clearly stronger than commodity peers.
4. Reject companies whose pipeline is early, duplicative, weakly validated, or already covered by stronger retained platforms.
5. Reject post-COVID diagnostics, commodity biological materials, and weaker blood/vaccine copies unless they have a distinct moat.
