# 1. A-Share Integrated Internet Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `互联网-互联网服务-综合互联网服务`.

The group mixes central-SOE digitalization companies, 5G wireless technology companies, AI training-data vendors, smart-campus platforms, environmental IoT and carbon-data platforms, internet marketing services, small IDC/cloud providers, digital-content companies, and provincial digital-media companies. The review separates domain workflow and infrastructure scarcity from generic internet services.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. This group has no direct-watch companies in the first pass because every retained case still needs proof beyond a broad internet-services label.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have central-SOE customer access, telecom standards capability, AI training-data workflow, smart-campus installed base, or national environmental/carbon data-platform experience.
3. Reject marketing and e-commerce services, smaller IDC/cloud providers already covered by stronger data-center platforms, parent-backed digital-content businesses without infrastructure scarcity, and provincial digital-media companies without scalable platform moats.
4. A watched integrated internet-services company must show domain data, installed workflow, standards capability, customer access, or regulated platform experience that a funded entrant cannot quickly replicate.
