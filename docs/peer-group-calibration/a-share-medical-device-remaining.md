# 1. A-Share Remaining Medical Device Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for the remaining `医药生物-医疗器械-医疗器械` companies not already handled in the earlier high-end medical-device calibration.

The earlier calibration directly retained 迈瑞医疗, 联影医疗, and 惠泰医疗. This follow-up pass reviews the rest of the group using the same capability-first lens: clinical workflow, registration barriers, consumable recurrence, physician/hospital trust, device engineering, and whether larger peers could easily copy the product.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain platform medical-device companies with durable brand/channel, clinical workflow, and product breadth.
2. Retain boundary niches in blood purification, ophthalmology, endoscopy, sequencing instruments, medical aesthetics, implantable biomaterials, high-value components, and scarce healthcare packaging only when the niche is not already covered by stronger retained peers.
3. Reject low-barrier consumables, medical distribution, weaker IVD copies, orthopedics weaker than the selected retained case, and device companies whose value is mainly capacity rather than clinical or technical barrier.
