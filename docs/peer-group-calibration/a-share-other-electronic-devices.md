# 1. A-Share Other Electronic Devices Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电子设备-电子器件-其他电子器件`.

This peer group is mixed: electronic component distributors, heat-shrink and polymer materials, EMI and thermal-management materials, PCB/interconnect platforms, PMIC and MEMS chips, RF connectors, acoustic components, vacuum electronics, microwave devices, display technologies, portable energy-storage brands, fuses, and ordinary consumer-electronics components. The review applies company-level judgment rather than forcing every company into the label implied by the group name.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain companies when proprietary materials, global interconnect/PCB platforms, magnetic-material leadership, or cross-industry solar/storage capability creates durable barriers beyond ordinary component manufacturing.
2. Retain boundary cases only when they own a distinct hard-technology niche such as thermal/EMI materials, analog ICs, MEMS, RF connectors, electronic vacuum devices, microwave devices, PMOLED/e-paper, thermoelectrics, portable energy-storage brands, or circuit-protection components.
3. Reject distribution, ordinary functional parts, generic connectors, lower-end PCB capacity, accessories, project/service-heavy hardware work, and weaker copies when stronger retained peers cover the durable thesis.
4. Cross-industry capability overrides the current peer-group label; for example, 阿特斯 is judged under the solar/storage platform rule and 横店东磁 is judged as a magnetic-material plus photovoltaic company.
