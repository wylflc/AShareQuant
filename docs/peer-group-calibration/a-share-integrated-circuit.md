# 1. A-Share Integrated Circuit Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电子设备-半导体-集成电路`.

The group includes foundries, OSAT, advanced packaging, analog, RF, image sensors, memory/MCU, SoC, communications chips, GPU, FPGA, semiconductor IP, and many smaller fabless companies. Current profit is not the primary screen; the review focuses on process learning, IP, software ecosystem, customer design-in, strategic platform value, and whether capital alone can quickly reproduce the position.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain foundry and OSAT leaders when process/yield learning, fabs, customer qualification, and supply-chain coordination create durable barriers.
2. Retain strategic compute, GPU, FPGA, CPU/DCU, and communications-chip platforms even when current commercialization is early, if the capability would be hard to recreate quickly.
3. Retain boundary analog, RF, CIS, interface, memory, MCU, IP, and power niches only when the product and customer design-in are distinct.
4. Reject weaker copies in consumer chips, commodity packaging/testing, fragmented power-management chips, and undifferentiated fabless products.
