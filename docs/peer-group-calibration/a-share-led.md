# 1. A-Share LED Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电子设备-光电子器件-LED`.

The group mixes LED chip and compound-semiconductor platforms, global LED display platforms, Mini/MicroLED and packaging companies, LED driver and power component niches, ordinary lighting companies, city-lighting projects, weak LED display contractors, commodity chip/packaging followers, mixed lighting businesses, and ST cases. The review separates process and platform moats from commoditized lighting capacity.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own LED/compound-semiconductor chip leadership or global LED display platform capability with product, channel, and customer-reference barriers.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have Mini/MicroLED, packaging, backlight, LED driver, special optoelectronic, or focused display-export niches not fully covered by stronger retained peers.
3. Reject ordinary LED lighting, city-lighting projects, weak display contractors, commodity chip or packaging followers, ST cases, and mixed lighting businesses without clear category leadership or technology moat.
4. Treat LED demand cycles carefully: current margins are secondary, but commodity exposure is evidence against companies whose product can be replicated by stronger chip, packaging, or display leaders.
