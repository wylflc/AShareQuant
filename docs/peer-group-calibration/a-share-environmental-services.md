# 1. A-Share Environmental Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `公用事业-环保-环保`.

Environmental services include waste-to-energy concessions, municipal environmental platforms, hazardous-waste treatment, membrane technology, water treatment, ecological restoration, sanitation services, and project-based engineering. The industry often has low organic growth and weak pricing power, so high scores from local concessions or project size are not enough.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain only the strongest concession/operator platforms with visible operating discipline and cash-flow quality.
2. Retain boundary cases when the company owns differentiated membrane technology, hazardous-waste qualification, or high-quality concession assets.
3. Reject ordinary water-treatment engineering, ecological restoration, sanitation, recycling, and project-contracting companies when the business is bidding-driven or easy to replicate.
4. Treat retained environmental companies as utility-like watch candidates, not high-growth compounders.
