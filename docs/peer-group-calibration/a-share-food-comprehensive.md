# 1. A-Share Food Comprehensive Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `食品饮料-食品-食品综合`.

The group mixes packaged food brands, frozen food, pet food, yeast and fermentation ingredients, health supplements, food additives, animal-protein processors, bakery and prepared foods, snack retailers, regional old brands, commodity agricultural processors, and ST cases. The review separates durable brand/channel/process moats from low-barrier packaged-food execution.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own category-defining consumer mindshare, national channel density, pricing power, vertical supply-chain control, or fermentation/process capability that capital alone cannot quickly replicate.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have a narrower but real brand, ingredient-process, foodservice-channel, pet-food, or vertical supply-chain niche not fully covered by stronger retained peers.
3. Reject low-barrier packaged food, regional baked goods, snack retailers, ordinary prepared-food processors, commodity agricultural products, weak brands, ST cases, and companies whose category can be attacked easily by new brands or channels.
4. Do not treat food "technology" like semiconductor technology; for companies such as 涪陵榨菜, the moat is brand/category mindshare, channel, pricing power, and operational consistency.
