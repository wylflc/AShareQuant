# 1. A-Share Cogeneration And Heat-Power Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `公用事业-电力-热电`.

The group mixes local cogeneration utilities, industrial-park steam suppliers, municipal heating companies, heat-service and energy-saving operators, and mixed local-development or materials companies with incidental heat-power assets. The review treats this as a local utility group rather than a high-growth energy group.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. This group has no direct-watch companies in the first pass. Even high-quality heat utilities are mostly local infrastructure assets with limited compounding.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have visible heat-network exclusivity, industrial customer density, multi-park operation, energy-saving retrofit capability, or a differentiated biomass/fuel niche.
3. Reject mixed companies where heat power is incidental, old municipal heating companies with weak operating quality, small local heat utilities already covered by stronger peers, and local development/trade/lease mixes.
4. Local incumbency is not enough. A watched heat-power company must show a durable pipe-network or park-level customer lock-in that a funded entrant cannot easily bypass.
