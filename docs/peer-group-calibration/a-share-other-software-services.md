# 1. A-Share Other Software Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `信息技术-计算机软件-其他软件服务`.

The group is even broader than industry application software. It includes embedded OS services, cybersecurity, cloud and ICT infrastructure, database, AI vision, document software, smart logistics, industrial control, digital government, financial IT, traffic IT, and IT outsourcing.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain companies that are actually product, infrastructure, workflow, or hard-domain platforms.
2. Retain boundary companies only when their niche is distinct and not already covered by a stronger retained platform.
3. Reject software-outsourcing, system-integration, project-delivery, generic smart-city, or replaceable IT-services companies even when they have technical staff and visible revenue.
4. Reject weaker cybersecurity, cloud, AI, and financial-software copies when the retained platforms already cover the main watchlist theses.
5. Defer aviation, traffic-control, and defense-cyber cases that need a separate rule.
