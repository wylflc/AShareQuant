# 1. A-Share Other Internet Services Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `互联网-互联网服务-其他互联网服务`.

The group mixes cybersecurity, visual-content copyright databases, financial IT, cloud infrastructure, internet-cafe/cloud-gaming platforms, internet healthcare, industrial internet, public-safety software, commodity-data services, digital reading, network visibility, mobile data, advertising/MCN/live commerce, IT outsourcing, system integration, and games. The review separates product/data/platform moats from low-barrier platform-dependent traffic services.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own hard-to-replicate security platforms, content/IP databases, regulated vertical software platforms, data assets, or infrastructure products with customer validation and switching costs.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have a differentiated cloud, financial IT, industrial internet, network visibility, public-safety, commodity-data, reading/content, mobile-data, or domain-software niche not already fully covered by stronger peers.
3. Reject low-barrier advertising, MCN, live-commerce operators, generic IT outsourcing, weak game/content hit businesses, platform-dependent traffic services, and system integrators without product or data lock-in.
4. Do not reward internet labels by themselves; the company must control an asset, workflow, regulatory qualification, protocol knowledge, vertical data set, or installed base that capital and traffic buying cannot quickly replicate.
