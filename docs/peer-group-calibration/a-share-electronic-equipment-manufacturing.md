# 1. A-Share Electronic Equipment Manufacturing Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for the residual A-share `电子设备-电子设备制造-电子设备制造` peer group.

This group is broad and mixes laser equipment, optical sensing, additive manufacturing, connectors, storage modules, EMS, consumer-electronics components, smart controllers, and some defense-electronics names. The review therefore does not use a single numeric cutoff. It applies the prior calibrated standard: retain companies only when capital alone is unlikely to reproduce the company's process know-how, customer qualification, product reliability, IP, or hard-to-replicate platform position.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct anchors when they are clear platform leaders or category leaders.
2. Retain boundary companies when they hold a distinct hard-technology niche not already covered by a stronger retained peer.
3. Reject useful but replaceable manufacturers when the business is mainly EMS, consumer-electronics assembly, smart-control outsourcing, low-margin component production, or application project delivery.
4. Reject weaker copies when a stronger retained company already covers the same process step or attention slot.
5. Defer defense-electronics and defense-computing companies when public evidence is insufficient to compare them fairly with civilian electronics companies.
