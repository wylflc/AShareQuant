# 1. A-Share Chemical Formulation Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `医药生物-化学制药-化学制剂`.

The group includes innovative-drug platforms, branded OTC/specialty pharma, anesthesia/CNS/ophthalmology niches, generic-drug manufacturers, and weaker formulation companies. Current profit is not the core screen; the review focuses on innovation, specialty clinical channel, brand trust, integrated manufacturing, and whether the company has durable advantages beyond generic-drug capacity.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain leading innovative-drug platforms when clinical, regulatory, commercial, and pipeline evidence supports durable capability.
2. Retain specialty-drug or branded-OTC companies when category focus, physician/channel trust, and product economics are hard to replicate.
3. Retain boundary cases only when they have a differentiated specialty franchise, innovation proof, or integrated capability not fully covered by stronger retained peers.
4. Reject generic, duplicative, weakly validated, volume-based-procurement exposed, or commodity formulation companies.
