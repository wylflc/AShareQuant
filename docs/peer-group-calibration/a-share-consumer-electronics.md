# 1. A-Share Consumer Electronics Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电子设备-消费电子设备-消费电子设备`.

The group mixes global EMS/ODM platforms, terminal brands, cross-border consumer-electronics brands, optical-display leaders, AI/5G modules, high-end audio, legacy TV and home-appliance groups, distributors, accessories, structural parts, and ordinary smart-terminal manufacturers. The review separates companies with durable platform, brand, customer-validation, semiconductor, or system-integration barriers from companies whose revenue scale mainly reflects manufacturing volume or channel distribution.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain consumer-electronics companies when global platform scale, leading-customer qualification, semiconductor/system capability, or differentiated terminal ecosystem creates barriers not easily bought with capital.
2. Retain boundary cases only when a company owns a distinct brand, module, optical-display, high-end-audio, edge-AI, or digital-manufacturing niche that is not fully covered by stronger retained platforms.
3. Reject ordinary ODM/EMS, accessories, structures, legacy consumer electronics, distribution, and low-barrier component suppliers when stronger retained peers already cover the durable thesis.
4. ST status, product-cycle pressure, or current profit volatility is not an automatic rejection reason; the core question is whether the company has a hard-to-replicate capability or merely a replaceable manufacturing/channel role.
