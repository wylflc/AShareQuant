# 1. A-Share Communication Transmission Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `信息技术-通信设备-通信传输设备`.

The group includes telecom equipment, optical modules, optical chips/devices, optical fiber/preform/cable, submarine cable, industrial IoT gateways, cellular modules, RF filters, integrated photonics, special communication, industrial networking, high-reliability connectors, test instruments, ceramic filters, and legacy communication-equipment vendors. The review focuses on communication infrastructure and AI/data-center optical interconnect barriers, not merely telecom-cycle revenue exposure.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain companies when they are global or national leaders in telecom equipment, optical modules, optical chips/devices, optical fiber/preform/cable, submarine cable, or AI/data-center optical interconnect infrastructure.
2. Retain boundary cases only when they own distinct industrial IoT, cellular module, RF filter, integrated photonics, special communication, industrial networking, high-reliability connector, test-instrument, ceramic filter, network-security, or high-speed interconnect capability not fully covered by stronger leaders.
3. Reject legacy network equipment, ordinary fiber/cable capacity, base-station antenna hardware, project/system integration, weaker optical-module followers, and low-barrier communication components when stronger retained peers cover the thesis.
4. AI/data-center optical links, deep-sea optical networks, industrial networking, and special communication are treated as separate capability vectors; one strong vector can justify boundary retention even when the company is not broad-based.
