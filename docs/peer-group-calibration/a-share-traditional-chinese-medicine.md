# 1. A-Share Traditional Chinese Medicine Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `医药生物-中药生产-中药生产`.

TCM companies can have real moats through scarce formulas, time-honored brands, category leadership, pricing power, OTC/channel trust, and evidence-based modernization. The group also contains many regional or single-product companies whose products are visible but whose durable moat is weaker.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain national or category-defining TCM brands with pricing power, formula scarcity, channel trust, or strong consumer mindshare.
2. Retain modern TCM companies when clinical evidence, prescription-channel depth, or product franchise strength is materially differentiated.
3. Retain boundary OTC/consumer-health niches only when the category position is clear.
4. Reject regional, duplicative, weak-brand, or policy/product-cycle-exposed TCM companies even when current sales are visible.
