# 1. A-Share PC, Server, And Hardware Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `信息技术-计算机硬件-PC、服务器及硬件`.

The group mixes global security and video-IoT platforms, AI-server and data-center hardware, strategic domestic CPU architecture, domestic computing and trusted-hardware ecosystem exposure, printer and office-hardware ecosystems, commercial-vehicle video niches, consumer PC brands, legacy flash-storage IP stories, EMS manufacturing services, ST cases, and mixed technology holdings. The review separates platform and technology moats from ordinary hardware manufacturing.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they have global video-security and machine-vision platforms, global video-IoT/channel reach, AI-server scale, data-center customer adoption, or strategic domestic CPU architecture and software ecosystem barriers.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have domestic trusted-hardware ecosystem exposure, domestic printer/office-hardware channels, or commercial-vehicle video and fleet-safety niches, but weaker scale or product depth than direct leaders.
3. Reject consumer PC brands, legacy IP stories without current product leadership, EMS/manufacturing-service companies, mixed technology holdings, and ST hardware companies.
4. A watched hardware company must show product/ecosystem control, software or algorithm capability, customer lock-in, data-center or government-enterprise channel position, strategic architecture, or global route-to-market. Assembly scale alone is not enough.
