# 1. A-Share Specialized Computer Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `信息技术-计算机硬件-专用计算机设备`.

The group mixes high-performance computing and servers, automotive diagnostic terminals, payment terminals, LED display-control systems, financial self-service equipment, cryptographic hardware, defense embedded computers, barcode/AIDC devices, special printers, rail and transport terminals, data-storage products, video surveillance, and IT integration. The review separates product platforms with ecosystem or qualification barriers from ordinary peripherals and project-based hardware.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own compute-infrastructure platforms, global diagnostic terminal ecosystems, LED display-control systems, payment/recognition platforms, commercial-cryptography hardware, or defense embedded-computing qualifications that capital cannot quickly replicate.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have a differentiated payment-terminal, financial/urban self-service equipment, barcode/AIDC, LED display-control, tax/e-government, intelligent-transportation, storage-safety, data-storage, command-center, rail-monitoring, or GNSS niche not already fully covered by a stronger retained peer.
3. Reject ordinary financial/cash-handling equipment, low-barrier peripherals, display modules, video-surveillance followers, IT/system integrators, project-based transport terminals, and weaker legacy hardware vendors.
4. Current profitability is secondary for scarce compute, cryptography, storage, or defense embedded-computing capability, but low normalized margins help identify commodity peripherals and system-integration businesses.
