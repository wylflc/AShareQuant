# 1. A-Share Discrete Semiconductors Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电子设备-半导体-半导体分立器件`.

The group mixes power-semiconductor IDMs and designers, IGBT and SiC module platforms, HVDC thyristor suppliers, diode and rectifier manufacturers, probe-card and probe-station companies, semiconductor test-equipment suppliers, photonics companies, memory-chip designers, auxiliary semiconductor-equipment subsystems, and mixed transition stories. The review separates hard semiconductor qualification and process know-how from ordinary discrete-device capacity.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they have scarce HVDC/UHV power-device capability, global or national IGBT/SiC module leadership, vertically integrated power-discrete IDM strength, advanced MOSFET platforms, or a differentiated global photonics platform.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have narrower but hard-to-copy positions in MEMS probe cards, thyristors, non-volatile memory, high-performance power devices, 12-inch probe stations, advanced-node equipment components, power-semiconductor test systems, or domestic high-power thyristors.
3. Reject transition stories, ST cases, mature diode and rectifier suppliers, auxiliary equipment subsystems, broad but non-leading power-discrete IDMs, small fabless analog/protection-device suppliers, weaker memory duplicates, and commodity discrete-device manufacturers.
4. A watched semiconductor company must show customer qualification, reliability validation, process know-how, product architecture, advanced equipment/component qualification, or global niche positioning. Revenue scale alone is not enough.
