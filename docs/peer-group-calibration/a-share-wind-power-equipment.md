# 1. A-Share Wind Power Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电气设备-电源设备-风能`.

The group mixes wind-turbine OEMs, wind towers, offshore foundations, wind main shafts, castings, composite wind parts, and companies whose wind label is incidental to renewable-power operation. The review separates real equipment capability from ordinary capacity exposure.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they are clear wind-turbine OEM platforms with technology, project references, large-turbine iteration, service base, manufacturing system, and market share.
2. Retain boundary cases when they have offshore wind technology, global wind-tower and offshore-structure customer qualification, global manufacturing footprint, or critical forged-component process capability.
3. Reject renewable operators with incidental wind labels, ordinary wind castings, duplicate tower and foundation manufacturers, and composite-part suppliers without category leadership.
4. Wind equipment has structural growth but weak industry economics. A watched company must show something harder to copy than capacity: turbine design, offshore references, grid/project validation, global customer qualification, process yield, service installed base, or unusually strong manufacturing system.
