# 1. A-Share Solar Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电气设备-电源设备-太阳能`.

Solar has strong long-term demand but severe manufacturing cyclicality and repeated overcapacity. The review therefore does not treat current losses or cycle trough margins as automatic rejection reasons, but it is stricter about whether a company owns a hard-to-replicate cost, process, technology, customer, bankability, or platform advantage.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain solar companies when they are global leaders in power electronics, photovoltaic equipment, photovoltaic materials, wafers, polysilicon, or integrated modules with cost, process, technology, customer, and bankability barriers.
2. Retain boundary cases only when they own a distinct inverter niche, next-generation cell technology, high-barrier equipment, low-cost upstream process, or specialty material platform not fully covered by stronger leaders.
3. Reject ordinary photovoltaic capacity, EPC/system integration, station operators, late pivots, weak module copies, and low-barrier components when stronger retained peers already cover the durable thesis.
4. Photovoltaic cycle pressure is expected; the key question is whether the company can survive and compound through cycles because it sits on a superior cost curve, owns hard process know-how, or controls a scarce customer/category position.
