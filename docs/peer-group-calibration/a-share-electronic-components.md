# 1. A-Share Electronic Components Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电子设备-电子元件-电子元件`.

The group contains PCB, copper-clad laminate, passive components, high-reliability military components, connectors, optical components, RF modules, power semiconductor modules, MEMS sensors, and many weaker duplicate manufacturers. The core question is whether the company owns process know-how, customer qualification, material formulation, reliability record, or high-end product mix that a well-funded entrant cannot quickly copy.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain PCB/material leaders only when they are exposed to high-end boards, package substrates, AI server boards, high-frequency/high-speed processes, or upstream materials.
2. Retain passive and high-reliability component leaders when reliability certification, military/new-energy customer qualification, and process know-how create durable barriers.
3. Retain boundary optical, RF, MEMS, power-electronics, and protection-component niches only when they are not weaker copies of retained leaders.
4. Reject duplicate PCB capacity, consumer-electronics components, distributors, trading companies, and capital-intensive display/component businesses without clear pricing power.
