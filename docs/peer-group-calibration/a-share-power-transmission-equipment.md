# 1. A-Share Power Transmission Equipment Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `电气设备-输变电设备-其他输变电设备`.

The group mixes UHV/high-voltage switchgear, full transmission platforms, transformers, tap changers, low-voltage electrical products, module-level photovoltaic inverters, high-voltage insulators, surge arresters, smart distribution, transmission towers, distribution cabinets, relays, and diversified or ST companies. The review prioritizes grid-core qualification, safety validation, long-cycle project references, and power-electronics/product platforms over ordinary fabrication or distribution-equipment scale.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain direct-watch companies when they own UHV/high-voltage core equipment, full transmission platforms, transformer or tap-changer leadership, inverter/power-electronics platforms, low-voltage electrical brands, or grid-qualified insulation/components that a capital-only entrant cannot quickly replicate.
2. Retain boundary cases when they own a differentiated high-voltage switchgear, grid-protection, insulation, power-electronics, renewable transformer, special cable/component, or smart-distribution niche not already fully covered by a stronger retained peer.
3. Reject transmission towers and steel structures, ordinary distribution equipment, low-barrier relays and components, weaker transformer followers, ST cases without scarce technology, and businesses whose grid label is diluted by unrelated activities.
4. Treat current cyclicality as secondary for grid-core equipment, UHV qualification, and inverter platforms, but use poor normalized margins and fabrication/project exposure as evidence of weak pricing power.
