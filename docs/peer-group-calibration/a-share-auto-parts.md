# 1. A-Share Auto Parts Peer-Group Review

## 1.1 Purpose

This note records the company-by-company review for `交运设备-汽车-汽车零部件`.

The group is large and includes intelligent cockpit/ADAS, brake-by-wire, chassis and drivetrain, thermal management, automotive glass, lighting, safety systems, lightweight die-casting, seat/interior, sensors, machining, stamping, molds, wiring harnesses, and many capacity-driven manufacturers. The review focuses on whether global OEM validation, safety-critical certification, software/electronics platform depth, or hard manufacturing process creates a durable barrier.

## 1.2 Retention Standard

1. Retain platform leaders in intelligent vehicle electronics, safety-critical braking/chassis, powertrain, automotive glass, thermal management, and lighting.
2. Retain boundary companies only when the niche is distinct: safety systems, lightweighting, EV power electronics, drivetrain, sensors, or globalized auto interiors.
3. Reject companies whose advantage is mainly OEM relationship, machining, stamping, assembly, local capacity, or low-margin manufacturing.
4. Treat legacy fuel-powertrain leaders as boundary or monitor cases when the moat is real but the technology route is under transition pressure.
